Our Story

Habibi Clothing was born in the streets of Pretoria.

What started as a small dream in the township yards and dusty streets of Gauteng has grown into a full streetwear movement. We saw young people hungry for clothing that represented their pride, their hustle, and their unique style — so we created it.

Every piece is designed with the spirit of Pretoria in mind — bold, resilient, and unapologetically authentic. From the hoodies that kept us warm during winter grind sessions to the tees that turned heads in Sunnyside and Hatfield, Habibi is more than clothing.

This is identity. This is culture. This is Pretoria.

Wallahi, we don’t follow trends.
We create for the ones who move different.
